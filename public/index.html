About WAEJUC

Universitas Negeri Malang invites you to participate in the International Enrichment Program – Western Australia East Java University Consortium (WAEJUC) in Perth, Australia which will be held on September 1 – 8, 2019. The host universities are The University of Notre Dame, The University of Western Australia, Curtin University, Edith Cowan University and Murdoch University. WAEJUC International Enrichment Program combines Academic study with cultural excursions to iconic Perth locations. This Program includes Academic, Social and Cultural Component as follows


  • Academic Component: Includes specialist lectures and interactive workshops at the five Western Australian universities.

  • Social and Cultural Component: A specially designed social and cultural program showcases the best of Perth and provides opportunities to interact with Western Australian university students.

Application

Eligibility

  • 1. Undergraduate students of Universitas Negeri Malang
  • 2. Nominated by the Office of International Affairs of Universitas Negeri Malang
  • 3. Having TOEFL ITP or TOEFL IBT score ≥ 477
  • 4. Commit to all aspects of the program upon the nomination
  • 5. Be physically and emotionally capable of completing the programs both in the host university and the home university

Required Documents

  • 1. CV (in English)
  • 2. Motivation Letter (in English; 200 words)
  • 3. TOEFL ITP or TOEFL IBT certificate (minimum score is 477 or equal)
  • 4. Academic Transcript (all semesters for students)
  • 5. Recommendation Letter (template could be found at: http://bit.ly/Recom_Letter_IEP_WAEJUC)
  • 6. Scan of Passport (must be valid 1 year from 8 September 2019)
